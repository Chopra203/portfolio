Canada’s World Cup 2022 Analysis Report

December, 2022
Nacsport and MS Office

In collaboration with Alberta FA, I played a crucial role in producing a detailed performance report for Canada’s World Cup 2022 team. This project was part of a unique initiative by Solent University's Football Studies program, where students analyzed and informed Canada Soccer’s performance at the World Cup in Qatar.

Process and insights:
  • Data Collection and Analysis: Leveraged match analysis and data collection to provide comprehensive insights. Utilized advanced tools and methodologies to evaluate team performance, identify strengths and weaknesses, and offer actionable recommendations.
  • Strategic Recommendations: Presented data-driven insights to Canada Soccer, aiding in their future planning and performance improvement. These recommendations were tailored to enhance team strategies and player development.
  • Continued Collaboration: The success of this collaboration has led to ongoing support for Canada FA. Over the next year, the insights gathered will be applied towards co-developing a coaching curriculum and supporting coach education courses.
  • Impact: This project provided significant strategic recommendations that contributed to Canada's overall performance. It also facilitated knowledge exchange at the highest level of international football, benefiting both the students involved and Canada Soccer.
